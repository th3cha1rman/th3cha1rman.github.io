Profile
======
Driven by a lifelong curiosity for how systems work, I now apply that same mindset to penetration testing identifying weaknesses, demonstrating impact, and giving organisations a clear view of their real security posture. I use practical, adversary emulated attack methods across networks, applications, and infrastructure, always aiming to stay ahead of adversaries.

I am actively involved in the cybersecurity community, regularly attending and contributing to local events, giving talks at 0xCoffee, and volunteering at BSides.
